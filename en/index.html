EVENT HISTORY

Highlights

Collaborations with municipalities, companies, and organizations across Japan and the world.

2025.10

Guest appearance at World Expo 2025 Osaka

Featured as "Artist Active on the World Stage." Drew GPS art of the Expo mascot over Osaka.

2025.7

Tsunagare JAPAN! — 3,700km across 21 prefectures

Completed 113-day run through 21 prefectures, drawing "Runner" GPS art to support Noto earthquake recovery.

Peru Project — 4,600km "Alpaca Geoglyph"

90 days across Peru. Scammed $3,000, attacked by wild dogs, stranded at 4,000m — completed the artwork anyway. Covered by media in 7 countries.

24 consecutive full marathons around Taiwan — 1,135km

A monumental project to connect Japan and Taiwan across generations.
